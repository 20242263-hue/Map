California stretches from foggy Pacific coasts to sun-drenched valleys and towering redwoods. It’s the birthplace of global entertainment, tech innovation, and a lifestyle that blends outdoor adventures with creative energy.

What to Expect

Expect variety: surf culture along the coast, vineyards inland, and high-energy cities like Los Angeles and San Francisco. Food trends start here; you’ll find fresh produce, fusion cuisine, and health-forward dishes everywhere.
